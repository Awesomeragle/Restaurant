Tenders Description

Our mission is to combine all natural ingredients with that home style cooking we all love. Tenders was founded in 1993 and we have been serving our world renound chicken ever since. Our chicken is free range and humanely slaughtered because we understand if you treat a chicken well, it will taste good too. All of our ingredients are bought locally to help support local business, like us. We employ only the best cooks to take these fresh ingredients and turn them into the home cooked meals you've come to expect from us. But who are these cooks? They are your mom and dad, grandparents, aunts and uncles- whoever you think of when you enjoy a home cooked meal. We believe that love is just as important an ingredient as our chicken.
